About

My name is Pranav and I am currently the sole author of this blog. My passion for home espresso brewing started several years ago, around the same time as I started my job at a company that retails equipment and roasts coffee. Everyone there is passionate about coffee, and why not? coffee is amazing! Now, After years of home espresso brewing, I decided to start this blog so I can share my passion about espresso with the world, as I continue to experiment with it and learn how to make great coffee.
